About

We’re the Ghana Climbing Association! We’re a group of climbing enthusiasts from different backgrounds all living in Ghana. With different levels of experience, we are passionate about promoting climbing in Ghana and providing access to climbing activities and training.

Through events like the climbing festival, we want to foster a sense of unity among climbers, inspiring a love for the outdoors and nurturing a growing climbing community in Ghana. The association envisions a future where climbing becomes widely embraced by locals, empowering individuals and fostering a vibrant climbing culture across the nation. We recognize the economic opportunities that a thriving climbing community can bring, promoting adventure tourism and supporting local businesses.

Location

Nestled in the Eastern Region of Ghana, the climbing festival's chosen location, Asubone, is a true gem for climbers. With its breathtaking views overlooking the Volta Lake and a plethora of unexplored cliffs and boulders, this spot is a paradise for adventure seekers.

Asubone offers a diverse range of rocks catering to climbers of all levels, providing a perfect playground to test skills and push boundaries. Surrounded by nature's beauty, this untapped treasure trove promises an unforgettable experience where climbers can form lasting memories and embrace the spirit of camaraderie amidst the thrill of conquering new heights.

Event Activities

Climbing Competition

This climbing contest is designed to test the climbers' skills, strength, and technique as they take on various challenging routes set up by experienced route setters. Participants will have the chance to compete, learn, and grow as climbers while being rewarded for their dedication and achievements. The competition will undoubtedly be a highlight of the festival, making it an unforgettable experience for all attendees.

Workshops

Workshops provide an avenue for climbers to learn and share knowledge. Beginner climbers can receive guidance on essential techniques, safety measures, and climbing fundamentals. Advanced climbers can benefit from workshops that focus on more specialized skills, advanced techniques, and training strategies. This knowledge-sharing environment fosters growth and helps climbers improve their skills and confidence.

Free Water Soloing

The rock climbing festival will offer an exciting opportunity for participants to try free water soloing. Free water soloing is a form of climbing where climbers ascend rock faces without the use of ropes or protection, but over bodies of water. Climbers can explore and climb these routes without the need for traditional climbing equipment, relying solely on their climbing skills and techniques.

Yoga

There will be daily yoga sessions from a certified yoga instructors who understand the specific needs and physical demands of climbers. The tailored sessions will cater to climbers of all levels, ensuring that beginners and experienced climbers alike can benefit from the practice.

Social & Community
Impact

Jobs

Organizing the festival will directly create job opportunities in the local area: we will need staff for event planning, setup, cooking, clean up, etc. As visitors come to the area for the festival, local businesses in food and drinks services will experience a surge in demand and revenue. And we want to build a campsite that will allow the Asubone community to host visitors the whole year!

Boosting Tourism

Similar to how the near town of Nkawkaw has become popular for Paragliding, we want Asubone to get recognition as a rock climbing destination, attracting more visitors and businesses in the future. It is also a great spot for mountain biking and hiking!

Community Development Fund

A Community Development Fund will be set-up (with your support!) to help young people in Asubone with educational initiatives and skills development programs. The establishment of a community development fund ensures our commitment to the long-term development and well-being of Asubone. The community has said they want to use such a fund to equip the health center, grow the public library and later build an ICT center to bring new skills and opportunities in the village.

Preserving Nature

We want the festival and the development of climbing in Asubone to strengthen the community’s awareness of the value provided by nature in their beautiful area. The festival will include awareness-raising activities like beach clean-ups and we will endeavor to leave as small a trace as possible on the environment.
